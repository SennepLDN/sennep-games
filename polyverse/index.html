Polyverse

The puzzle game that helps you switch off without switching off your brain. Travel through the Polyverse and build worlds using the magic of simple math and strategy.

Travel through the Polyverse with Sumi

SUMI is a curious explorer from beyond the stars, dreaming of discovering new worlds. There's just one small problem. SUMI only understands things through numbers. That's where you come in. By solving puzzles, you help SUMI make sense of the Polyverse. With every structure you build, SUMI's understanding grows... and so does her heart 💛

Build new worlds

Build charming dioramas one piece at a time. Just start by adding one number to another and see the world take shape over time. Choose from a myriad of worlds, from natural habitats and familiar locations to fictional worlds and extra terrestrial places.
